About me

Obsessed with
AI that actually works

I'm an AI/ML developer from Anand, India, currently in my third year of B.Tech Information Technology. The thing that keeps me up late isn't just building models — it's the challenge of making them fast, deployable, and genuinely useful in the real world.

My path into AI wasn't through a course — it started with a question: can I replace my mouse with just my hand? That first computer vision project unlocked everything. I've been building ever since, moving from gesture control to NLP pipelines to exploring the frontier of large language models.

What drives me is the gap between research and production. I want to close it — writing systems that are as clean and precise as the research they're based on, but also fast enough to run in the real world.

Build AI systems that solve physical, real-world problems
Bridge the gap between ML research and deployment
Eventually contribute to frontier AI safety and alignment

Computer Vision 2024

Gesture Mouse Control

Replaced a physical mouse entirely with hand gesture recognition running at real-time frame rates. The system tracks 21 hand landmarks via webcam and maps finger positions to OS-level cursor events, including smooth scroll via custom C-types integration. Zero additional hardware required.

Computer Vision 2024

Smart Volume Control V3

A gesture-based audio controller that translates the Euclidean distance between your thumb and index finger into a continuous OS volume level. Live visual feedback overlays keep the interaction legible. Third iteration, with significant latency improvements over V1 and V2.

OpenCV MediaPipe Pycaw NumPy

NLP 2025

Auto Resume Screening

An automated NLP pipeline that ingests raw résumés, extracts structured features, scores candidates against job descriptions, and returns ranked shortlists. Eliminates the manual bottleneck in early-stage hiring. Built with classic ML algorithms and a clean, interpretable scoring model.

AI Journey

How I got
to here

2023

Started B.Tech IT

Anand, India

Began formal computer science education. Quickly realized coursework was the floor, not the ceiling. Started self-directing deep into ML research in parallel with standard curriculum.

Early 2024

First Computer Vision Project

OpenCV · MediaPipe

Built Smart Volume Control V1, then V2, then V3. Learned the hard way about latency, frame rates, and why real-time ML is a completely different problem than offline ML. Shipped something that actually worked at 30fps.

Late 2024

Gesture Mouse — Full System

CV + Systems Programming

Built the culmination of the CV work: a complete mouse replacement. Required dropping into ctypes for OS-level cursor control. The first time I bridged ML inference all the way down to hardware interaction.

2025

Shifted to NLP

HuggingFace · Transformers

Moved from spatial/visual AI into language. Built the Auto Resume Screening pipeline. Started reading transformer papers seriously. Experimented with fine-tuning smaller LLMs for classification tasks.

2026 — Now

Full-Stack AI

End-to-end · Deployment · Agents

Engineering complete applications from model to interface. Exploring RAG systems, agentic AI, and MLOps. The goal is shipping AI systems that don't just work in notebooks — they work in production.
